✅ Your next step: request your domain

To get your website live, set up your domain in Free For Charity’s Cloudflare account — it’s free. Choose the option that fits your charity:

  • 👉 Register a new .org domain — we register a brand-new .org for your charity.
  • 👉 Transfer a domain you already own — move your existing domain into our Cloudflare (a few extra steps on the order form).

On the order form you tell us the exact domain, the contact details, and your agreement that FFC will register/transfer and manage it in Cloudflare.

Once you submit it, our volunteers register the domain in Cloudflare, point it at your new site, and manage the DNS for you. After that, any domain or DNS change is just a support ticket away — please don’t change domain settings elsewhere, as it can take your site or email offline.

What comes after your domain

As an approved 501(c)(3) you qualify for the nonprofit email programs. Once your domain is set up, we’ll build your website on GitHub Pages — and then, because the nonprofit email programs require an active website to qualify, help you set up your charity email (Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace). Our 501(c)(3) setup guide is at https://freeforcharity.org/501c3/; you can text Clarke Moyer at 520-222-8104 anytime with questions.
